School Overview

Honorato Guzman Baquiran (HGB) College is a private educational institution in Tumauini, Isabela. Since its foundation in 2005, the school provides quality education that builds graduates who are competent, active, and adaptive in navigating problems in both the local and global communities.

HGBaquiran College is recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and the Department of Education (DepEd). The school mainly offers undergraduate programs in Teacher Education, Business Administration, Criminology, Information Technology, and Hotel and Restaurant Management.

Mission

HGBaquiran College commits itself to offer quality education that would develop a well-rounded human being who possesses desirable values, attitudes, and skills thereby enabling him/her to be locally competitive, live a productive life, cope with the social and moral challenges of the modern world, and contribute to nation-building.

Vision

A well-rounded human being. Equipped with the values, attitudes, and skills that will enable him/her to be locally and globally competitive, live a productive life, contribute to nation building, and meet creatively and resourcefully the social and moral challenges of the world.

Core Values

Dedication, Diligence, Determination, Devotion, and Discipline
